# Favorite Existing Apps - Categorize and Evaluate
## 1. Instagram
**Category**: Social
**Mobile:** uses features like the camera, location, push notifications, and real-time engagement, focuses on mobile-first content creation, with features like Stories, Reels, and Live streaming that encourage on-the-go usage.
**Story:** app's value is clear: it allows users to capture, edit, and share moments instantly while engaging with others, appeals to most people, especially younger demographics
**Market:** massive global audience; casual users, influencers, businesses, and content creators
**Scope:** Stripe down verison can be about just photo sharing, for better developement and more engagment features like DMs, Stories, and Reels are valuable

## 2. TickTick to do list
**Category**: Productivity
**Mobile:** has features like push notifications, real-time syncing, voice input, location-based reminders, and widgets
**Story:** helping users organize their lives efficiently, simplifies task management
**Market:** market is large yet competitive, there are already many to-do list apps available, huge value to niche groups like students
**Scope:** well scoped app with balance between simplicity and advanced features, it requires syncing, scheduling logic, notification management, and offline support, strip down of this app is basic to-do list

## 3. Reminder app
**Category**: Productivity
**Mobile:** Mobile focused app, has features like push notifications, add, delete, calendar usage, alerts, location based alerts
**Story:** never forget important tasks, easily set quick, one-time, or recurring reminders
**Market:** large generic market, pretty similar apps designed by MS, Apple, Google keep etc.), can be used by students, professionals, elderly users, and health-conscious individuals.
**Scope:** technicially simple to make with timers, notifications, and calendar settings. strip down is basic alert notifier. 

## 4. Notes
**Category**: Education
**Mobile:** More usabale for Ipad but can be also used on phones. Providing features liek voice dictation, handwriting recognition, image embedding, and cloud syncing.
**Story:** Write notes and easily access it at your fingertips at any time. 
**Market:** Large market but most of them are not free. Notability, Apple Notes (Free), Good notes, Collanotes. Users are students, professors, and learners connected to education
**Scope:** Complex for ipad version but stripe down version can be a platform allowing users to create "notebooks" and type their notes and thoughts. Features include text, font, scan etc.

## 5. Alarm
**Category:** Productivity
**Mobile:** Sound notifcations alerting users. Sync with calendar, helps user with time sensitive events. Introduce some wake up activities such as shaking the phone which utilizes the phone's gyroscope. 
**Story:** ensure you wake up on time and not just snooze forever. With our innovative activities that requires the user to complete them before shutting off the alarm, the user will ensure they stay awake. 
**Habit:** The app is designed to create good sleeping habits. However, users may open the app just a few times a day. Mainly when they wake up and before they sleep. If a user has a good schedule, they may only open the app to shutdown the alarm. 
**Market:** Huge market of people with deadlines to meet. Especially those who are heavy sleepers or forever snoozers. People are unlikely to pay for an alarm app but will settle for by ads. Some similar alarm apps include Alarmy.
**Scope:** The base of the app (alarm feature) is well documented and is the more basic form of it. Where our app pushes the boundaries of an alarm app comes with its activity. Most of the project's work will be in utilizing all of the phone's features such as gyroscope, typing, and visuals to ensure the user is awake.
